Baro Energy Trading

Baro Energy Trading is a company established in December 2, 2018 GC and registered under the Ethiopian Commercial Registration and Business License Proc No. 980/2016. The major destinations are the most commercial areas of the country like Gambela, North-West Ethiopia, Semera, Addis Ababa, Bure, and Mizan-Tepi…. However, the company’s head office is located in Addis Ababa.

The company has been building good reputation on the fuel, oil, and lubricants supply industry in Ethiopia. Adopting new and innovative technologies to improve the logistics and deliver the highest quality services to its clients are the company’s dignifying characteristics. With operations that embrace many parts of Ethiopia, we strive to be the company of choice for clients, partners, customers, and employees.

Besides, in the past six years; the company has been expanded its business ventures to:

  • Importing engine oils and lubricants
  • Importing Fuel dispenser machines and accessories
  • Importing Vehicle Spare Parts Procurement and Supplies;

    • Besides, in the past six years; the company has been expanded its business ventures to:

    • Importing Industrial and Agricultural Chemicals, fertilizers
    • Exporting Agricultural commodities (i.e. Oil crops and Pulses, Coffee)
    • Production of industrial, commercial and food crops including cotton, sesame, soybean, green mung bean, maize and sorghum on over 4000 hectares.
    • Collect over 50, 000 MTs of agricultural products annually from both own farms and through the contract farming.
    • We’re working with more than 2,000 small and medium scale farmers in Humera, Gambela, Welaita, Shewa-robit and Benishangul…areas.
    • Wholesale and Retail Trade…
    • Transportation and Logistics Services
    • Machinery Rental Services
    • Manufacturing sector like textile, Mining and Food processing sectors…(ongoing projects)
