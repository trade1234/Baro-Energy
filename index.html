    About Us

    Baro Energy Trading

    Baro Energy Trading is a company established in November 23, 2011 EC and registered under the Ethiopian Commercial Registration and Business License Proc No. 980/2016. The major destinations are the most commercial areas of the country like Gambela, North-West Ethiopia, Semera, Addis Ababa, Bure, and Mizan-Tepi…. However, the company’s head office is located in Addis Ababa.

    The company has been building good reputation on the fuel, oil, and lubricants supply industry in Ethiopia. Adopting new and innovative technologies to improve the logistics and deliver the highest quality services to its clients are the company’s dignifying characteristics. With operations that embrace many parts of Ethiopia, we strive to be the company of choice for clients, partners, customers, and employees.

    SERVICES

    Imported Petroleum Products

    refer to various types of fuel, such as diesel, gasoline, heavy and light fuel oil, kerosene, and jet fuel, that are sourced from international markets and supplied for transportation, industrial, and energy needs.

    Exported Agricultural Commodities

    Exported Agricultural Commodities are high-quality crops, such as sesame seeds, soybean seeds, green mung bean seeds, pulses, and raw coffee beans, grown locally and traded in international markets, contributing to the global supply of agricultural goods.

    Machineries and Inputs

    Machineries and Inputs encompass essential equipment and supplies, such as fuel dispenser machines, agricultural machinery, and agricultural inputs, used to support fuel distribution, farming operations, and overall agricultural productivity.

    Imported Engine Oils and Lubricants

    Imported Engine Oils and Lubricants are specialized fluids sourced from international markets, designed to reduce friction, enhance performance, and protect engine components in vehicles, industrial machines, and agricultural equipment.
